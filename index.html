GeoGuessr

Find the hidden province by guessing on the map or typing its name.

Game Rules

  • Goal: To find the hidden city in the fewest guesses.
  • Time Limit: You have 60 seconds to win the game.
  • Guess Allowance: You lose 1 life every 3 incorrect guesses. You have 3 lives total.
  • Feedback: After each guess, you will see the distance (km) to the city.
  • Map Colors (Proximity): The map uses a reverse color scale where Dark Green (Closest) ‚Üí Red (Farthest). Colors get greener as you get closer to the hidden province.
